Who should complete this survey?

This survey is designed for HR Directors, Finance Directors, MDs, CEOs, or anyone responsible for setting or approving salaries within their organisation. If that's more than one person at your company, only one response per organisation is needed.

Much of the salary data available to Oxfordshire businesses is either too broad, too generic, or out of date.

This survey is different.

It is built specifically for Oxfordshire employers, capturing real, current salary data across key business functions, covering our specialist areas of expertise in:

PA & Administration
Human Resources
Finance
Marketing

The result will be The Oxfordshire Salary Guide — providing business leaders with insight into what's actually happening on the ground, rather than national averages or assumptions.

The survey takes around 10 minutes to complete.
